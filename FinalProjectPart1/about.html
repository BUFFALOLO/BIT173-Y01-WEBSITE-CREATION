ABOUT US

Our first stream was April 24, 2022 and we became Twitch Affiliates on August 23, 2022. Matt and I would spend 8+ hours on a Saturday gaming together and one day he looked at me and said, "We should stream on Twitch", I responded with "What would our name be?". After throwing ideas back and forth Matt suggested "Couch Campers", which I replied "YES"!. I quickly created our Twitch account along with all other social media accounts and we began the journey of figuring out how to go live. One thing we have read and now tell other aspiring streamers, the hardest part is clicking live that first time, once you do it, it's easier every time. Our setup was not the greatest our first couple streams but we did trial and error until it was decent. We have grown a community of amazing people who love to have a good time and enjoy each others company. We are very chaotic but love gaming together. We currently mainly stream Apex Legends but like to branch out or play other games once we are burned our after 6+ hours.

ABOUT MATT

Matt started playing games at a young age of every genre but has always found himself playing first person shooters. Gaming is his biggest hobby. He games to have fun and to relieve the stress after a long day of manual labor as an electrican. He loves trying new games and can finish a storyline quickly as he fully emerses himself into it. He has created bonded friendships and continues to.

ABOUT LOLO

Lauren grew up watching her older brother play video games and would dabble a little herself but never really fully emersed herself into that world. In middle school and high school, Dance Dance Revolution was big and would play that at sleepovers with her friends. She also found Guitar Hero being a great game as she could play this competively with her brother. She was always interested in gaming but as she grew older didn't have any friends that did. She bought a PS4 but all it did was sit there and collect dust. During COVID while quaranting with Matt she would watch Matt play games and he could tell she was gaining interest. One day she asked "Can I play with you?" and Matt quickly responded "YES", and the rest was history.
